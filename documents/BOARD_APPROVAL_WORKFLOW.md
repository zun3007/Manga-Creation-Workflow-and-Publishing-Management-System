# Board Chapter Approval Workflow

## Workflow

1. Mangaka submits a chapter.
2. Tantou Editor reviews the chapter.
3. The chapter becomes EDITOR_APPROVED.
4. Editorial Board approves or rejects the chapter.
5. Approved chapters become BOARD_APPROVED.
6. Only BOARD_APPROVED chapters can be scheduled.
7. When the release date arrives, the chapter becomes PUBLISHED.

## Status flow

DRAFT
→ IN_PROGRESS
→ READY_FOR_EDITOR_REVIEW
→ EDITOR_APPROVED
→ BOARD_APPROVED
→ PUBLISHED

## Rejection flow

EDITOR_APPROVED
→ IN_PROGRESS
